Your Security Is Important To Us

We know that your privacy is very important to you. When you place orders on our Sites, all of your order information, including your card number and delivery address, is transmitted through the Internet using Secure Sockets Layer (SSL) technology. SSL technology causes your browser to encrypt your order information before transmitting it to our secure server. SSL technology, an industry standard, is designed to prevent someone other than operators of our Sites from capturing and viewing your personal information.

To view our complete "Privacy and Security Statement," please click here. It will tell you about the manner in which we collect, use, disclose and otherwise manage the personal information you entrust to us at any of our The Home Depot store locations, through any The Home Depot Call Centers, through The Home Depot websites or through our authorized service providers.
