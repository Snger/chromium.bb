How does Pre-Order work?
Pre-Order allows you to order an item before it becomes available in stores. When it becomes available, it's automatically shipped and may arrive on, or up to several days from, the Release Date.

What is the Release Date?
The Release Date is the date the publisher chooses to release the item and make it available in stores. Release dates are subject to change by the publisher and changes will be reflected on the Web Site.

Will the price change?
We guarantee that your Pre-Order price will not change before it is shipped.

Will my credit card be charged for my Pre-Order?
If you use a credit card as payment, the Pre-Order amount will not be charged until the item is shipped.

What if I use a gift card as payment?
You cannot use gift cards for payment at this time.
